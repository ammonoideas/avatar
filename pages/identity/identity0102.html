THE SKELETON OF THE FOUNDER

"This is our founder." The voice of the Recordkeeper echoes softly through the vast, cavernous halls, in response to your yet unspoken question.

"It was his mission that led to the construction of the Bibliopelagia, and it is by his direction that it was left in my care. His aim was to slowly but surely accumulate and preserve as much of his own experiences as possible.


"I believe it was in an effort to ensure his immortality."
