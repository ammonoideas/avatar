LOST REALM

Here, everything becomes something else.

The small and unassuming magnolia tree that curls over a bench becomes a picturesque glade in a fantasy forest where fascinating creatures emerge, and each of its silky petals is a treasure in and of itself.

The gnarled roots of the larger tree are a treacherous but not unconquerable domain of adventure. The rusted fence that lines the old baseball diamond becomes spectacularly ancient in your eyes.

The wooden bench that stands faithfully beside it, weathered planks splitting from one another and curling in on themselves, becomes a magnificent diving board.

The concrete-floored notch cut into the school is a quiet reprieve from the elements and a space for storytelling.


But this is not that place as it was.
