You could spin some artsy tale about how you came down here in search of inspiration, but down here, next to the water, that sort of fabrication seems unnecessary.

In front of you lies the unadorned truth: there is no deeper meaning to be found by plumbing these shallow depths. There is only sympathy, the kindred spirit one can find in the numbing breath of the winter air and the emptiness of the water beneath the nighttime streets.


Everything feels clear down here. (return to the Hall of Sensation)
